Malappuram

City Of Hills

Malappuram district has a rich and vibrant history. Malappuram has made numerous contributions to the cultural heritage of Kerala. A famous centre for Hindu-Vedic learning and Islamic philosophy, the temples and mosques of this region are well known for their spectacular festivals.long with historic monuments and diverse natural attractions, a range of cultural and ritual art forms add to its charm as a destination.
