Alappuzha

Venice Of The East

Alappuzha is a district of immense natural beauty. Embraced by the Arabian Sea in the west and a network of lakes, lagoons and freshwater rivers criss-crossing it, this backwater country is home to a vibrant and avian life. By virtue of its proximity to the sea, the town has always carved out an exclusive place for itself in the maritime history of Kerala.A land of lush paddy fields referred to as the 'Rice Bowl of Kerala'.
