Thiruvananthapuram

The capital of Kerala

Thiruvananthapuram is located in the south-western tip of India, bounded by the Arabian Sea in the west and Tamil Nadu in the east. Named after Anantha Padmanabha or Lord Vishnu, the city is home to many ancient temples. The wooded highlands of the Western Ghats in the eastern and northeastern borders give some of the most enchanting picnic spots in Thiruvananthapuram.
